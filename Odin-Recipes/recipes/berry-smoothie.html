Berry Smoothie

Description

A refreshing, vibrant, and nutrient-packed smoothie that makes for a perfect quick breakfast or afternoon snack. It's sweet, creamy, and takes almost no effort.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries (strawberries, blueberries, raspberries)
  • 1/2 cup plain or vanilla Greek yogurt (for creaminess and protein)
  • 1/2 cup milk (whole, almond, or oat milk all work great)
  • 1 tablespoon honey or maple syrup (optional, for extra sweetness)

Steps

  1. Load the blender: Add the frozen berries, Greek yogurt, milk, and sweetener (if using) into your blender cup. Always put the liquid in first to help it blend smoothly.
  2. Blend until smooth: Secure the lid and blend on high speed for about 30 to 45 seconds until the mixture is completely smooth and there are no large fruit chunks left.
  3. Check the consistency: If the smoothie is too thick, add a splash more milk and blend for another 5 seconds. If it's too thin, add a few more frozen berries or a couple of ice cubes.
  4. Pour and enjoy: Pour the smoothie into a tall glass, grab a straw, and enjoy immediately while it's cold.
