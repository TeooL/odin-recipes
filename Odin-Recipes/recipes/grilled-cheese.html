Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

The ultimate comfort food. This recipe delivers a perfectly golden-brown, crispy exterior with a rich, gooey, melted cheese center. It's quick, foolproof, and deeply satisfying.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white or sourdough bread
  • 1-2 tablespoons unsalted butter (softened at room temperature)
  • 2 slices of American or Cheddar cheese (or a mix of both)

Steps

  1. Prep the bread: Spread the softened butter evenly on one side of each slice of bread. This will be the outside of your sandwich.
  2. Assemble the sandwich: Place one slice of bread, butter-side down, in a cold non-stick skillet. Place the cheese slices on top, then cover with the second slice of bread, butter-side up.
  3. Cook low and slow: Turn the heat to medium-low. Cook for about 3 to 4 minutes until the bottom bread is beautifully golden brown and crispy.
  4. Flip and melt: Carefully flip the sandwich over. Cover the skillet with a lid for 2 to 3 minutes—this helps the cheese melt completely while the second side browns.
  5. Serve: Remove from the pan, slice diagonally, and serve while the cheese is still warm and stretchy.
